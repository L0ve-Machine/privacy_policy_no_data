Privacy Policy

Average Calculator
Last updated: January 30, 2025
Important: This app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information whatsoever. Your privacy is fully protected as all data remains locally on your device.

1. No Personal Information Collection

This application operates with a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No tracking of user behavior or app usage
  • No analytics or user data collection
  • Complete offline operation - all calculations performed locally

2. Local Data Storage Only

The app stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Numbers you input for calculations (stored locally only)
  • Language preference setting (stored locally only)
  • This data never leaves your device
  • No synchronization or cloud storage

3. Advertisements

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Please note:

  • Ad services may collect device information for ad personalization
  • Advertising identifiers may be used
  • Ad interactions may be tracked by the ad provider
  • We do not control or access this advertising data

For Google's privacy practices: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Internet Permissions

This app requires internet access solely for displaying advertisements. No other data is transmitted over the internet by our application.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected:

  • There is no risk of personal data breaches
  • No user data is stored on external servers
  • Your calculation data remains private on your device

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy:

Twitter/X: @K_Isa1

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date. Given our no-data-collection approach, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.
