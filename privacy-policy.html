Important: These apps do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information whatsoever. Your privacy is fully protected as all data remains locally on your device.

These applications operate with a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted

2. Local Data Storage Only

The apps store minimal data locally on your device:

  • App settings and preferences (stored locally only)
  • User data and content (stored locally only)
  • This data never leaves your device
  • No synchronization or cloud storage

3. No Internet Access Required

These applications are designed to work completely offline:

  • No internet connection required for any functionality
  • No data is ever transmitted over the internet
  • No external servers are contacted
  • Complete privacy and security through offline operation

4. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected:

  • There is no risk of personal data breaches
  • No user data is stored on external servers
  • Your app data remains completely private on your device

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date. Given our no-data-collection approach, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.
